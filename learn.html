Form 1 Algebra Notes

Welcome to Form 1 Algebra! Master the basics and build a strong foundation.

1. Algebraic Expressions

Algebraic expressions are mathematical phrases that can include numbers, variables, and arithmetic operations.

Examples:

  • 3x + 5
  • 4a - 7
  • 2m² + 3m - 5

Simplifying Algebraic Expressions

To simplify an algebraic expression, combine like terms by adding or subtracting their coefficients.

Example: 3x + 5x = 8x (Combine 3 and 5)

Example: 4a - 2a + 7 = 2a + 7 (Subtract 4 and 2)

Tips for Mastering Algebra

1. Break down complex expressions into smaller parts.

2. Always combine like terms to simplify your work.

3. Check your answers by substituting the variable back into the equation.

4. Practice makes perfect – keep solving problems to gain confidence!
