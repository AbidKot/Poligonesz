Form 1 Algebra Notes

Algebraic expressions are mathematical phrases that include numbers, variables, and operations.

Simplifying Algebraic Expressions

To simplify an algebraic expression, combine like terms by adding or subtracting their coefficients.

Example: 3x + 5x = 8x (Combine the coefficients 3 and 5)

Solving Linear Equations

Isolate the variable on one side of the equation and perform inverse operations to simplify.

Example: 3x + 5 = 20
Subtract 5: 3x = 15
Divide by 3: x = 5
